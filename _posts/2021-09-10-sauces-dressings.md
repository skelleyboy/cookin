## Tzatziki

### Ingredients

* 1 grated cucumber 
* 1 ½ cups plain Greek yogurt
* 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
* 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint and/or dill
* 1 tablespoon lemon juice
* 1 medium clove garlic, pressed or minced
* ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
