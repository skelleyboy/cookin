# Bara (fried bread)

## Ingredients 

1. 3 cups flour
2. 1 teaspoon cumin
3. ½-1 teaspoon turmeric
4. 2 teaspoons active or dry yeast
5. 1/2 teaspoons salt
6. ½ teaspoon white pepper
7. 1 ¼ cup warm water or more adjust water to form soft dough
8. 1 Tablespoon sugar
