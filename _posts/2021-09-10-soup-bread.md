# Soup

* 1 onion 
* 1 carrot 
* 1 tomato
* 1 celery  stalk
* 1 garlic bulb
* frozen peas
* white wine or vinegar 
* handfull cabbage or kale
* olive oil

# Bread

* 1 pound all-purpose flour (about 3 cups) plus more for sprinkling
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 3/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
* 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water
